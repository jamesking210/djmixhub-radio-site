About the station

The story behind DJMixHub.com

A personal project with a public radio feel.

Why it exists

Jimbo created this site and station for fun as a free way to feature DJs from all around the world and their different music and mixing styles.

The goal was simple: build something that feels more like a real radio brand than a backend panel, and give DJs a clean place to be heard.

Built on Docker and AzuraCast

DJMixHub.com is hosted with Docker and powered by AzuraCast, making it portable, easy to redeploy, and easy to keep improving over time.

Project repo: github.com/jamesking210/djmixhub-radio-site

Want to connect?

We are actively looking for new DJs to join the station and submit mixes to us. If you have a sound, a style, or a set that deserves ears, reach out.
