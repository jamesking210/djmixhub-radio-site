About DJMixHub

A simple station with real personality. No corporate vibe. No bloated app. Just a good-looking place to listen and discover DJ mixes.

This is an always free radio station. Jimbo created DJMixHub for fun as a free way to feature DJs from all around the world and the different music and mixing styles they bring.

The station runs on Docker and AzuraCast, with a lightweight front-end made to feel like a real internet radio page instead of a dashboard.

It is built to stay easy to maintain, easy to host on GitHub, and easy to grow over time as more DJs and mixes get added.

Want to connect?

We are actively looking for new DJs to join the station and submit mixes. If you have a sound, a style, and a reason people should hear your set, send it over.
