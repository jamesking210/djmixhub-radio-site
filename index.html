About DJMixHub

A station that feels personal on purpose

This is an always free radio station. Jimbo created DJMixHub for fun as a free way to feature DJs from all around the world and show off their different music taste, mixing style and overall vibe.

The station is hosted with Docker and AzuraCast, and the site is intentionally simple: clean, stylish, fast and easy to keep updated on GitHub.
