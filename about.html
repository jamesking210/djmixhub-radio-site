About the station

The story behind DJMixHub.com

DJMixHub.com was created by Jimbo for fun as a free way to feature DJs from all around the world and showcase their different music tastes, mixing styles, and energy.

Why it exists

The goal is simple: give DJs a clean home on the web where listeners can tune in, discover new talent, and keep the music going without digging through a boring control panel or generic player page.

DJMixHub.com is hosted with Docker and AzuraCast, making it easy to self-host, easy to update, and easy to grow over time.

Built on open tools

This site is part of an openly managed project so it is easy to deploy and improve. You can view the website repo here: github.com/jamesking210/djmixhub-radio-site.

The station itself lives at radio.djmixhub.com, powered by AzuraCast.

Want to connect?

We are actively looking for new DJs to join the station and submit mixes from anywhere in the world. If you have a sound, a style, or a set you want featured on DJMixHub, reach out and introduce yourself.

Contact: jimbo@djmixhub.com
